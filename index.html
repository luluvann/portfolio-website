01. About Me

My interest in web development started back in 2019 when I tried to automate pictures renaming process using Python and command lines and when I became comfortable with using SQL queries, querying large databases of thousands of lines. When I was a Business Operations Analyst, the aspect of my job I was enjoying the most was to analyze data, find that little detail that would solve a problem. After I self taught most of the technical skills thanks to all the amazing resources available online, I decided to enroll in the Coding Bootcamp of the University of Toronto to hone those technical skills I acquired and also to give me the opportunity to practice my collaboration skills in a context of development using agile methodology. I completed the bootcamp in July 2021. I also acquired 2.5 years of work experience in SaaS company as a front-end developer where I also put into practice both my back-end and front-end development skills.
